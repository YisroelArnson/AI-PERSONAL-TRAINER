# COACH_PRINCIPLES.md

App-owned coaching standards. This is where the product defines what "good coaching" means.

## Recommended Structure

### Safety First
- Injury and pain posture.
- When to downshift.

### Coaching Standard
- Simplicity, clarity, and adherence.

### Progression
- How to think about overload, volume, and regression.

### Exercise Selection
- Preferred substitution logic.
- Equipment and constraint handling.

### Readiness And Recovery
- How stress, soreness, sleep, and schedule should affect recommendations.

### Communication
- How much explanation to give.
- How to ask follow-ups.

## V1 Notes
- Keep this stable and product-owned.
- Do not store user-specific preferences here.
- Do not turn this into a second system prompt. It should be domain guidance, not runtime mechanics.
